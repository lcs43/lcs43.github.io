WATER CONSERVATION CONFERENCE 2000

Sponsored by
New Mexico State University
City of Las Cruces
Elephant Butte Irrigation District
New Mexico Water Conservation Alliance

Location: Las Cruces Hilton, 705 Telshor Blvd.

Topics include:

. Water Management Administration and adjudication
. City of Las Cruces Water Plan
. History of Drought in New Mexico
. New Mexico Draought Plan
. Making Water Conservation Work (Case Studies)
. Planting Grass With Less Water
. Drip Irrigation in Desert
. Reuse of Wastewater for Irrigation
. Remote Sensing and Water Conservation
